What is Lorem Ipsum?

Lorem Ipsum is a type of placeholder text often used in the printing and publishing industry to fill temporary spaces in a layout or design. It is used as a placeholder for the actual text that will be inserted later. The main characteristic of Lorem Ipsum is that it resembles readable text, but in reality, it is a sequence of words and letters without apparent meaning.

The term "Lorem Ipsum" has origins in classical Latin literature, specifically in the work "De finibus bonorum et malorum" by the author Cicero, dating back to the 1st century BC. The modern version of Lorem Ipsum was popularized in the graphic industry in the 16th century when an unknown printer scrambled the words from a passage of "De finibus" to create a type of sample text.

Due to its semantically null nature, Lorem Ipsum is useful for designers, developers, and editors who need to visualize the layout of a page without being distracted by real content. Additionally, it allows evaluating the text flow and overall appearance of a page without the need for meaningful text.
